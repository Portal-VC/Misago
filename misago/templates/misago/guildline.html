COMMUNITY GUIDELINE

Whether you are here to ask question or to solve others problems, you must follow these rules to ensure a better community – a community where everyone can get their problems solved regardless expertise or identity.

OUR EXPECTATION

+ When asking a question, you should make it easy to understand. The title should be meaningful and the description should perfectly explain what the problem is. The title and description must match together.
+ Always be nice when answering others question.
+ Avoid making joke about the question or the person who’s asking the question.
+ When giving the answer, you should include clear explanation of why the problem occur
and what we need to do in order to fix it with code explanation (*if needed).
+ Always be open when receiving feedback from the question you asked or the answer you gave.
+ Always remember to vote the best answer as a way to say thanks you while making others easier to find the answer that work for them.
+ If you use source code or any illustration from others, please give credits to them by mentioning their name or website.

UNACCEPTABLE BEHAVIOUR

+ Don’t post anything that goes beyond programming topic
+ Don’t post articles to promote or sell any product that doesn’t related to programming
+ Don’t use bad words, unfriendly or any language that offend others
+ Don’t bully or intimidate others by any means

ENFORCEMENT

Everyone can come together and contribute in building a better community. However, if you commit any unacceptable behavior, you will meet these consequences.

Warning

If this is the first time of your misconduct, moderators will remove the content and send you the warning message

SUSPENSION

If you repeatedly perform misconduct behavior, moderators will suspend your account depending on the level of the violation.

EXPULSION

Moderators will expel people who intentionally display a pattern of violating our guideline.
